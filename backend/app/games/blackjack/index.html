📖 How to Play

🎯 Goal

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

🃏 Card Values

  • 2–10 → Face value
  • J, Q, K → 10 points
  • A (Ace) → 11 or 1 (automatically adjusted)

🎮 Actions

  • 🃏 Hit — Draw another card
  • ✋ Stand — Keep your hand, dealer plays
  • ⏫ Double — Double your bet, get exactly one more card
  • ✂️ Split — Split two equal cards into separate hands (costs same bet)
  • 🏳️ Give Up — Surrender and get half your bet back
  • 🛡️ Insure — Side bet (half your bet) when dealer shows Ace; pays 2:1 if dealer has Blackjack

💰 Payouts

  • Blackjack (A + 10/J/Q/K) → pays 3:2
  • Regular Win → pays 1:1
  • Push (tie) → bet returned
  • Bust (over 21) → you lose

🏦 Dealer Rules

The dealer must hit on 16 or less and stands on 17+. The dealer hits on soft 17 (Ace counted as 11).

💡 Tips

  • Never hit on 17 or above
  • Double down on 11 vs dealer 2–10
  • Always split Aces and 8s
  • Surrender 16 vs dealer 9, 10, A
  • Stand on 12–16 if dealer shows 2–6
